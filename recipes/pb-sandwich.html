Peanut Butter And Honey Sandwich

Description

This delicious sandwich is famous for its easy preparation and healthy properties. Peanut butter is highly nutritious and full of various vitamins and minerals. It's also extremely easy to make at home and anyone can do it.

Another factor that makes a pb sandwich so good is that it can be enjoyed at any time. It can serve as both a breakfast as well as any other meal. You can also eat it alongside any other meal that you prefer so there's always a reason to enjoy a peanut butter and honey sandwich.

Ingredients

  • peanut butter (chunky or smooth)
  • bread of your choice
  • honey
  • cinammon (optional)

Steps

  1. prepare the bread slices by putting them side-by-side
  2. scoop up an appropriate amount of peanut butter
  3. spread it evenly across the surface of the first slice
  4. pour the desired amount of honey on the second slice
  5. spread it evenly
  6. carefully put the second slice with honey face down on top of the first slice with the peanut butter
